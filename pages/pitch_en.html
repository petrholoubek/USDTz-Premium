Banking & Non-Banking Cooperation

Banking Sector — Institutional Integration

  • On-chain settlement rails integrated with core banking via APIs and permissioned channels
  • USDt.z as regulated collateral for bilateral lending and repo facilities
  • Custody partnerships & proof of reserves for regulatory compliance
  • Liquidity provisioning agreements to ensure tight spreads and market depth

Key value: seamless fiat-grade liquidity and compliance.

  • Programs for startups: collateralized credit lines & incubation grants

Key value: fast adoption, capital efficiency and product innovation.

High-Yield Programs (HYP)

Our HYPs are structured, auditable yield programs designed for institutional and professional counterparties. Each program includes a clear risk framework, reporting, and configurable risk tiers.

HYP — Market Making Pool

Provide liquidity to our main pool and earn spread + performance fees. Automated risk controls and rebalancing reduce inventory risk.

Target yield: 6–12% APY (variable)

Apply

HYP — Collateralized Credit Line

Institutional credit facilities backed by USDt.z collateral; tailored terms, on-chain transparency and off-chain KYC for counterparties.

Target yield for lenders: 4–8% APY

Request Terms

HYP — Yield Vaults

Managed strategies aggregating treasury yields, DEX farming and lending protocols. Strong emphasis on diversification and audits.

Target yield: 8–18% APY (strategy-dependent)

HYP — Institutional Staking Programs

Long-term staking programs with tiered rewards and governance participation. Ideal for strategic partners and foundations.
