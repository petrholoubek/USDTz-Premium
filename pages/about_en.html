About USDt.z Token

USDt.z is a next-generation stablecoin combining the stability of traditional currencies with cutting-edge blockchain technology.

Why We Created USDt.z

Our goal was to create a secure, transparent, and reliable token that facilitates the use of cryptocurrencies in both banking and non-banking sectors, offering stability comparable to traditional money.

Key Features of USDt.z

  • 1:1 fixed USD backing
  • Supports collateral for loans, including for young clients without traditional guarantees
  • High security thanks to next-generation blockchain technology
  • Transparent and auditable system
  • Fast and low-cost transactions

Use Cases

USDt.z can be used across a broad range of financial applications, from loans and trading to liquidity provisioning and asset stabilization.

Support for Young Clients

The token is ideal for young clients and startups who often lack traditional collateral for loans. USDt.z enables using cryptocurrency collateral as a guarantee, opening new financing opportunities.
