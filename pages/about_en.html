About USDt.z Token

USDt.z is a next-generation stablecoin that combines the stability of traditional currencies with cutting-edge blockchain technology.

Why We Created USDt.z

Our goal was to develop a secure, transparent, and reliable token that facilitates cryptocurrency usage in both banking and non-banking sectors, offering stability comparable to traditional currencies.

Key Features of USDt.z

  • 1:1 fixed USD backing
  • Support as collateral for loans, including for young and emerging clients without traditional guarantees
  • High security thanks to next-generation blockchain technology
  • Transparent and auditable system
  • Fast and low-cost transactions

Use Cases

USDt.z can be utilized in a wide range of financial applications, from lending and trading to liquidity provisioning and asset stabilization.

Supporting Young Clients

The token is ideal for young clients and startups who often lack traditional loan collateral. USDt.z enables the use of crypto collateral as security, opening new financing possibilities.
