USDt.z Documentation

USDt.z is an innovative next-generation stablecoin designed for both banking and non-banking sectors. Below you will find detailed information about security, technical features, roadmap, and tokenomics.

Security

Security is our top priority. USDt.z is built on the robust Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with decentralized transaction validation.

The token undergoes regular audits by independent security firms, ensuring smart contracts remain tamper-proof.

Our own liquidity pool guarantees stability and immediate trading volume, minimizes pump and dump risks, and ensures transparency.

Roadmap

The USDt.z development plan includes continuous ecosystem expansion, implementation of new features, and increased adoption in the banking sector.

Key milestones:

  • Q3 2025: Integration with leading non-banking financial institutions
  • Q4 2025: Launch of smart contracts for loans and credit
  • Q1 2026: Expansion of liquidity pools and cross-chain interoperability
  • Q2 2026: Launch of own DEX and governance model

Chart: Roadmap implementation progress of USDt.z.

Technical Features and Tokenomics

USDt.z is an ERC-20 token fully compatible with Binance Smart Chain, ensuring low fees and fast transactions.

Tokenomics are designed to support stability, liquidity, and user trust:

  • Fixed 1:1 peg to the US dollar
  • Regular audits and transparent reporting
  • Own liquidity pool on PancakeSwap
  • Staking and governance capabilities
