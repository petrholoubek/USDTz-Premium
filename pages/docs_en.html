USDt.z Documentation and Audit

USDt.z is a next-generation stablecoin designed to provide security, transparency, and stability for both banking and non-banking sectors. This section provides comprehensive technical documentation, smart contract details, and the results of independent security audits.

Technical Documentation

The Whitepaper offers an in-depth explanation of the USDt.z tokenomics, underlying blockchain technology, and the innovative mechanisms that ensure a 1:1 peg with the US Dollar. It covers smart contract architecture, governance, and collateralization strategies that maintain the token’s stability.

Smart Contract and Security

USDt.z smart contracts have been developed with rigorous security standards and audited by leading blockchain security firms. The contracts implement multi-layered protection, including timelocks, multisignature wallets for treasury control, and automatic liquidity management to ensure resilience against attacks and market volatility.

Audit Report

The independent audit was conducted by a trusted third-party company, confirming the robustness and security of the USDt.z ecosystem. The audit validates the integrity of the token’s smart contracts and verifies that no critical vulnerabilities exist.
