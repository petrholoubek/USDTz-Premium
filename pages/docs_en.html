USDt.z Documentation

USDt.z is an innovative next-generation stablecoin designed for both banking and non-banking sectors. Below you will find detailed information about security, technical elements, roadmap, and tokenomics.

Security

Security is our top priority. USDt.z is built on the robust Binance Smart Chain (BSC) blockchain with decentralized transaction validation.

The token undergoes regular audits by independent security firms ensuring the integrity of the smart contracts.

Our dedicated liquidity pool guarantees stability and immediate trading volume, minimizing pump and dump risks while ensuring transparency.

Roadmap

The USDt.z development plan includes continuous ecosystem expansion, implementation of new features, and increasing adoption within the banking sector.

Key milestones:

  • Q3 2025: Integration with leading non-banking financial institutions
  • Q4 2025: Implementation of smart contracts for loans and credits
  • Q1 2026: Expansion of liquidity pools and cross-chain interoperability
  • Q2 2026: Launch of proprietary DEX and governance model

Chart: USDt.z roadmap implementation progress.

Technical Elements and Tokenomics

USDt.z is an ERC-20 token fully compatible with Binance Smart Chain, ensuring low fees and fast transactions.

Tokenomics is designed to support stability, liquidity, and user trust:

  • Fixed 1:1 peg to the US dollar
  • Regular audits and transparent reporting
  • Dedicated liquidity pool on PancakeSwap
  • Staking and governance capabilities
