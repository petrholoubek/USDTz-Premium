Token Usage

Banking sector: The token enables banks to use collateral for loans, accelerates transactions, and supports fair interest rates within decentralized applications.

Non-banking sector: Facilitates payments and transfers between individuals and businesses, supports microtransactions, and ensures instant liquidity for e-commerce and other online services.

Regulation & Compliance: The token complies with legal frameworks, supports AML/KYC processes, and allows banks and non-banking institutions to meet regulatory requirements.
