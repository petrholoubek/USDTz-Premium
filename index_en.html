About USDt.z Token

USDt.z is a stablecoin combining the stability of traditional currencies with next-generation blockchain technology.

The token provides high security, transparency, and wide usability across various financial applications.

The project was created in response to the need for a reliable token with a clear 1:1 peg to the US Dollar, suitable for banking and non-banking sectors.
